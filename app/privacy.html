Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

At The Hitter's Ledger, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address and name provided during sign-up via Firebase Authentication.
  • Performance Data: Baseball/softball statistics, logs, and metrics you manually enter into the ledger.
  • Payment Data: We do not store credit card details. All payments are processed securely by Stripe.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data specifically to:

  • Provide and maintain your personalized hitting ledger.
  • Process subscription payments and prevent fraud.
  • Send essential account notifications (password resets, billing updates).
Data Ownership: You own your stats. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal performance data to third-party advertisers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard providers to power the app:

  • Google Firebase: For data storage and user authentication.
  • Stripe: For payment processing and subscription management.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your information as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
